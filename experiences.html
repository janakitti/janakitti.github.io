FRC

The Student Activity Council at the Woodlands Secondary School is dedicated to enriching the school climate through special events by the students for the students. Members of council facilitate other school events to ensure that they run smoothly and successfully.

SAC

Team 4308: ABSOLUTE ROBOTICS is an FRC robotics team based at the Woodlands School in Mississauaga, Ontario. Our team consists of over 80 members from grades 7-12 who are passionate about the STEM. Our team became divisional semi-finalists at the Detroit World Championship the past year!

DECA

DECA is an international business competition that aims to prepare emerging leaders and entrepreneurs in marketing, finance, hospitality and management in high schools and colleges around the globe.

I, along with my partner, competed in the Entrepreneurship Start-up Business Plan category where we developed our own start-up business and prepared an 11-page report to pitch to a panel of judges. In 2018, we created Spark, a parking lot management system designed to reduce the amount of traffic on the streets of major cities due to ineffective parking systems. We became Provincial Champions with our product and competed at the international level (ICDC) in Atlanta, Georgia. In 2019, we created STRING, a visual-based productivity app that uses the visual analogy of strings and knots to improve productivity among students and employees. We also became Provincial Champions for this project.

SHAD

SHAD is an award-winning enrichment and entrepreneurship program and network that empowers exceptional high school students – at a pivotal point in their education – to recognize their own capabilities and envision their extraordinary potential as tomorrow’s leaders and change makers

I attended SHAD at the University of Waterloo campus in the summer of 2017.
